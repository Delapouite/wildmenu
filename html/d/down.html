down

CTRL-d CTRL-e CTRL-f see scroll in the user-guide

The ⇟ Page Down key is often hard to reach without leaving the home row or even absent on some laptop keyboards.

To scroll the window downwards, here are a few other chords:

  • CTRL-f → down a full screen
  • CTRL-d → down a half screen
  • CTRL-e → down the number of lines you specified as count

This triad is composed of 3 consecutive letters so it's an easy way to remember them.
