jump

What's the difference between a move and jump you may ask? Is jumping implies a more ample movement? Yes in a sense. But the distinctive feature which makes jumps useful is that they are recorded in the jumplist.

The current jumplist is displayed with jumps. It feels like a log of your recent itinerary produced by a GPS.

To move up and down this list: CTRL-o (out) and CTRL-i (in)
