up

CTRL-b CTRL-u CTRL-y see scroll in the user-guide

The ⇞ Page Up key is often too far away from the home row or even absent on exotic keyboards.

To scroll the window upwards, here are a few other combos:

  • CTRL-b → up a full screen
  • CTRL-u → up a half screen
  • CTRL-y → up the number of lines you specified as count

This trio forms the mnemonic word buy.
